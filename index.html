This is what We do

Currently travellers/backpackers do not have an efficient way to access information and messages in a new place. With digital eyewear devices (Google Glass /Hololens /Eyewear) becoming mainstream, Exploera will provide a novel location-based platform by using AR technology for travelers to explore nearby area and share information.
